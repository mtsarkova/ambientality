    Cold Ice is a UK non government organization, which is working towards global warming solution

    We aim to spread the knowledge about the global warming and consequences it is going to bring to an everyday life. One person can make a difference. Join our campaigns supporting research expeditions of Antarctica's flora and fauna! Our company was inspired back in 2007 by the National Geographic video and conference explaining the importance of the pollution.
